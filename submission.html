Submission Guidelines

Authors are encouraged to submit original research articles that align with the scope of the CNAHS Research Journal.

Eligibility

  • Submissions must be authored by students, faculty, or independent researchers in the field of health sciences.
  • The manuscript must be unpublished and not under review elsewhere.

Formatting Requirements

  • Manuscripts should be in Microsoft Word (.docx) format.
  • Use Times New Roman, 12pt font, double-spaced text.
  • Follow the APA (7th Edition) citation style.
  • Maximum length: 5,000 words, excluding references.

Submission Process

  1. Prepare your manuscript following the above guidelines.
  2. Attach your manuscript and a cover letter.
  3. Email your submission to journal@cit.edu with the subject line: "Research Submission - [Your Title]".

Note: Submissions will undergo a peer-review process, and authors will be notified within 2-3 weeks.
